Chapter One

Eighteen.
Eight.

18% Chromium
+
8% Nickel

SS Stainless

Eighteen percent chromium. Eight percent nickel. That exact ratio is what turns ordinary steel into stainless — sharper, stronger, built to last. It's the standard behind the instruments that demand the most: surgical tools, precision hardware, the kind of equipment where failure isn't an option.

It's also the science behind our name — and our promise. At 18|8, that same transformation applies to you: look better, feel better, perform better.

Since 2008, we've dedicated ourselves to reinventing the barbershop for the modern man. Because we believe a man's week goes better when there's an hour inside it that's just his. Not a fifteen-minute clipper job between errands. A real sit-down in a semi-private station — a conversation, a precision cut by someone who actually listens, a straight-razor shave, a hot towel, and a handshake on the way out.

It's the transformation the name promises. The kind that doesn't rust, doesn't dull, and doesn't rush.
