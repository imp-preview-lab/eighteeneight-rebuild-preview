The House

A Neighborhood
Original.

Tucked into Preston Hollow Village, our Dallas flagship has been the unhurried sit-down men drive across town for. Dark leather, warm light, a bar at the back, and a team of master stylists who have been sharpening their craft here for years. Walk in sharp. Sit in the chair. Leave sharper.

Our stylists know their regulars by name, know when you have a meeting on Thursday, and know exactly what the wedding photographer is going to want from you on Saturday. It's not a haircut. It's a standing appointment with the guy who gets it right every time.
